Common Pricing Objections & Responses

"It's too expensive"

Response: "I understand cost is a concern. Let's look at the value equation - what's the cost of not solving this problem?"

Follow-up: Present ROI calculator showing specific savings and benefits

"We don't have budget"

Response: "Budget constraints are common. What if I could show you how this pays for itself in 6 months?"

Follow-up: Explore payment options and phased implementation

"Competitor is cheaper"

Response: "Price is just one factor. Let's compare the total value and long-term benefits."

Follow-up: Present competitive analysis and unique value propositions

"Need to think about it"

Response: "Of course, this is an important decision. What specific concerns can I address right now?"

Follow-up: Identify specific objections and provide targeted information

"ROI isn't clear"

Response: "Let me create a customized ROI analysis based on your specific situation."

Follow-up: Use ROI calculator with prospect's actual numbers

"Not the right time"

Response: "I understand timing is important. What would need to change for this to be the right time?"

Follow-up: Explore urgency drivers and cost of delay
