Natural Conversation Techniques

Conversation Starters

For Cold Calls

"Hi [Name], I know you're busy, so I'll be brief. I noticed [specific trigger event] and thought you might be interested in how [similar company] solved [relevant challenge]."

For Follow-ups

"Thanks for taking the time last week. I've been thinking about what you said regarding [specific challenge], and I have some ideas that might help."

Conversation Bridges

Transitioning to Discovery

"That's interesting. Help me understand - what does that look like in your day-to-day operations?"

Moving to Solution

"Based on what you've shared, I think there's a way we could help. Would you like to see how [specific solution] addresses exactly what you described?"
